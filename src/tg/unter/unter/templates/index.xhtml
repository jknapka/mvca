Welcome to the Migrant Volunteer Coordinating Assistant

You are already registered and logged in. Please go to your volunteer information page and use the

You can register as a new volunteer and then tell us which days and times during the week you are available to help.

If you are already registered, you can log in to see your profile and any events you may be able to help with. We often need volunteers to

  • Drive migrants to the airport or bus station from local hotels and shelters.
  • Provide interpreter services for other volunteers and migrants (almost always English/Spanish).
  • Help prepare or serve meals for our local migrant population.

If you are an administrator, you can log in to create new events and send text or email alerts to volunteers who have availability at the appropriate times. You can also check the status of upcoming events and verify whether enough volunteers are available, and find the information needed to manually call, text, or email volunteers if needed.
